Frequently Asked Questions

How is my donation used?
Our donations are used for animal rescue operations, medical treatment, food, shelter maintenance, and staff training. We are transparent about our finances and provide detailed reports.
Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes! PawConnect is registered as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Your donation is tax-deductible. We provide receipts for all donations.
Can I donate monthly?
Absolutely! Monthly donations provide sustainable support for our programs. Contact us for recurring donation options.
Can I sponsor a specific animal?
Yes! Our sponsorship program allows you to directly support a specific animal. You'll receive updates and photos of your sponsored pet.
Is there a minimum donation amount?
No minimum amount! Every contribution matters. Even Rs. 100 can make a difference in an animal's life.
